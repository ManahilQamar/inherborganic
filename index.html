Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use Inherb Hair Oil?
For best results, we recommend using Inherb Hair Oil 2-3 times a week. Apply it to your scalp and hair, massage gently for 5-10 minutes, and leave it on for at least 2 hours or overnight before washing.
Is Inherb suitable for all hair types?
Yes, Inherb is formulated for all hair types - dry, oily, normal, and combination. Our natural ingredients work to balance your scalp's natural oils while nourishing your hair.
How long until I see results?
Most users notice improvements in hair texture and reduced hair fall within 2-3 weeks of regular use. For significant results in hair growth and thickness, consistent use for 2-3 months is recommended.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we only ship within Pakistan. We're working on expanding our shipping options to other countries in the near future.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened and unused products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund.
